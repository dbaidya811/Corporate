E-commerce Platform

×

Project Information

  • Client: GlobalRetail Inc.
  • Category: Web Development
  • Date: January 2023
  • Technologies: React, Node.js, MongoDB, AWS

Project Description

GlobalRetail Inc. needed a modern e-commerce platform to expand their online presence and streamline their sales process. We developed a custom solution that integrated with their existing inventory management system and provided a seamless shopping experience for their customers.

The platform features a responsive design, advanced search functionality, personalized product recommendations, secure payment processing, and a comprehensive admin dashboard for managing products, orders, and customer data.

Results

  • 40% increase in online sales within the first 3 months
  • 25% reduction in cart abandonment rate
  • Improved inventory management efficiency by 30%
  • Enhanced customer satisfaction with a 4.8/5 rating

Fitness Tracking App

×

Project Information

  • Client: FitLife
  • Category: Mobile Development
  • Date: March 2023
  • Technologies: React Native, Firebase, HealthKit, Google Fit

Project Description

FitLife wanted to create a comprehensive fitness tracking app that would help users monitor their workouts, nutrition, and overall health. We developed a cross-platform mobile application that integrates with wearable devices and provides personalized fitness plans and nutrition advice.

The app features workout tracking, meal planning, progress visualization, social sharing, and AI-powered coaching to keep users motivated and engaged.

Results

  • Over 500,000 downloads in the first 6 months
  • Average user engagement of 4.5 sessions per week
  • 85% user retention rate after 3 months
  • Featured in the App Store's "Apps We Love" section
